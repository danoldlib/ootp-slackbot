# OOTP Slack Bot Feature Ideas

Here is a list of potential features to add to the bot in the future:

### 1. 🚑 Devastating Injury Report
Losing a star player can change the landscape of the league. You could scrape the recent injuries and highlight the most impactful ones (e.g., players with a high WAR or out for 3+ weeks).
- **"Brutal Blows"**: "The Yankees lose Aaron Judge for 4 weeks (Strained Oblique)."

### 2. 🤝 Blockbuster Trade Analyzer
If the bot can parse the transaction logs, it could highlight major trades that occurred during the sim.
- **"Blockbuster Alert"**: If a player with a WAR over 3.0 is traded, the bot sends an alert breaking down the pieces moving on both sides.

### 3. 🧹 Sweep Watch & Rivalry Drama
Highlighting when a team completely dominates a series.
- **"Get Out the Brooms"**: "The Red Sox swept the Yankees in a 3-game series at Fenway, outscoring them 15-4!"

### 4. 🎩 Rare Feats (No-Hitters & Cycles)
While the game usually announces these, having the bot provide a dedicated, stylized highlight with the final out's box score or the player's stat line can be a great touch.
- You could also track near-misses (e.g., "Max Scherzer took a no-hitter into the 8th inning...").

### 5. 📉 The "Golden Sombrero" or "Rough Outing"
The inverse of the "Play of the Week". Highlighting a truly terrible performance (in a fun way).
- **Batter**: "Joey Gallo struck out 5 times in 5 at-bats."
- **Pitcher**: "A brutal day for the rotation: Starter X gave up 8 earned runs in just 1.2 innings."

### 6. 🚨 Walk-off Magic
Did any games end in a dramatic fashion? You can scan the box scores for games that ended with a walk-off hit or walk-off home run and highlight the hero.

### 7. 👶 Prospect Watch / Call-ups
If StatsPlus tracks top prospects, you could have the bot announce when a Top 100 prospect makes their major league debut during the sim.
- **"Welcome to the Show"**: "The Orioles have called up the #2 overall prospect, Jackson Holliday!"

### 8. ❓ Trivia / "Guess the Player"
Have the bot drop a blind stat line from the current season and challenge the channel to guess who the player is.
- "I'm hitting .315 with 12 HRs, but I have a miserable -1.2 dWAR. Who am I?"
